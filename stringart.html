String Art

The fascinating world of string art shows how you can create beautiful curves with lines! In this animation you get to choose the max point on the x and y-axes, the number of lines to plot and the speed of the plotting. With these parameters what curve do you get each time? Surprisingly, you get a parabola! This parabola is called the envelope of the given family of lines.
